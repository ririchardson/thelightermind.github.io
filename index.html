Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Is there anything I need to do to prepare for a hypnosis session?

A: While there is no special preparation for a hypnosis session, there are some do’s and don’ts and a few tips for success:

- Make sure you are not driving or are in a situation where your attention is required when starting your zoom session.

- Enjoy a relaxed routine before the session to be in a relaxed state (e.g. go for a short walk or meditate).

- Avoid coffee (unless it's your usual routine in the morning), avoid alcohol, and eat light.

- Wear comfortable clothes and try to ensure you won’t get interrupted during the session.

- Keep an open mind - set a clear intention for the session and keep in mind you get out of it what you put into it.

Q: How long will it take to lose weight after weight loss hypnotherapy?

A: The exact amount of time depends on each client as the reasons and habits are different for each person. In many cases, results are noticeable after a few sessions.
